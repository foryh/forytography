Behind the lens
Who's shooting

Based in the Treasure Valley,
at home in the backcountry.

Forytography is built around whatever's in front of the lens that week — a quiet alpine lake, a squirrel that won't hold still, a couple's portraits, or a drumline mid-performance under the stadium lights.

Alongside personal landscape and wildlife work, I volunteer as a photographer for the Boise Gems, covering rehearsals and performances throughout their season.
